MeetRegal Puffin

Bringing Quality Coffee to Bedford

With a focus on hospitality

Regal Puffin will be a neighborhood specialty coffee roaster and espresso cafe built for the fast paced commercial and residential mix of Bedford, Brooklyn. We’ll select choice, high quality, specialty green coffee beans to be artfully roasted, packaged, and sold. We will brew our coffee using intentional, cutting edge brew techniques to boast our beans’ distinctive character. Guests will receive a complimentary chocolate “crown” with each cup. Guests can also enjoy an assortment of in-store made croissants, fresh squeezed juices, and curated selection of Brooklyn-made goods.
